Bio

In my coding journey, my father, a professional in the field, played a pivotal role in igniting my interest in code from a young age, providing glimpses into his career and encouraging me to pursue it. Furthering my education at North Idaho College, I owe a significant part of my coding proficiency to Mrs. Miller, my computer science teacher. One memorable coding experience occurred during my second semester when, faced with a disastrous project, I navigated through challenges to produce a functional blackjack game, regretfully leaving some bugs unresolved. Competent in C++, I also find the freedom in coding with binary intriguing. Emphasizing clarity and conciseness, I believe simplicity is key in efficient code. Currently progressing through school, I have transitioned from a novice to a more knowledgeable coder, particularly through the exploration of Linux systems.
Looking ahead, my aspiration is to establish a managerial role in a company akin to my father's workplace, like General Atomics.
